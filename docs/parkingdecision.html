OFFICE OF THE COUNTY EXECUTIVE
EXECUTIVE OFFICE BUILDING
ROCKVILLE, MARYLAND 20850



IN THE MATTER OF:

PROPOSED CREATION OF RESIDENTIAL
PERMIT PARKING IN PARKWOOD_KENSINGTON RESIDENTIAL AREA


STATEMENT OF DECISION OF COUNTY EXECUTIVE

Having considered the Report and Recommendation of the Hearing Officer in the matter of the Residential Permit Parking Petition for the east side of Saul Road between Franklin Street and Oldfield Drive, for the reasons stated in the Report and Recommendation, it is my determination that the permit parking petition should be granted and a residential permit parking area created. The area in which residential permit parking is to be created is limited to the east side of Saul Road between Franklin Street and Oldfleld Drive as stated in the petition. This decision is to take effect in sixty (60) days. I am further asking that the Department make another effort to work with the Montgomery County Public Schools and the Montgomery County Planning Board to come up with a better solution that would alleviate the need for permit parking.


<signed>
Isiah Leggett, County Executive

November 16, 2007
