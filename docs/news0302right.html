Hey Moms!
Are you a stay-at-home or part-time working mom of a preschool child? Join the MOMs Club of Chevy-Chase/Kensington. Every month is filled with fun, inexpensive (more often free!) activities you and your kids can enjoy including playgroups, park visits, museum trips and more! Meet other Moms and allow your kids to socialize! Visit http://www.geocities.com/momsclubcck and attend the next meeting on 2/25/02 at 10:30am at the Kensington Library. Meetings are held the last Monday of each month.
