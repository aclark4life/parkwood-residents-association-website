Key Contacts for BRAC expansion of the
new Walter Reed at Bethesda

Tips on Talking Points:

  • Thank them for any support to date
  • Maintain a positive tone about the expansion
  • Mention where you live, how you know the area, and why you are concerned that more traffic and/or poor planning will negatively impact the base
  • This is a highly unusual project – critical mission, expansion in an urban area, a community that wants to see it work, not just a local issue but a national imperative to do it right
  • They should seek to use every legislative strategy at their disposal to make sure this works
